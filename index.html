Veritania

Veritania is a fictional European country. Farming, energy costs, and trust in government are the defining issues.

The government is preparing to pass the Living Landscapes Act, a law requiring 15% of agricultural land to be set aside for nature recovery by 2035.

The opposition is already campaigning against it.

The concerns

Across Veritania, three concerns keep coming up.

"Farming is getting harder"
"Everything is getting more expensive"
"Elites control everything"

The opposition is already using these concerns to turn people against the Act.

Every message has four parts

MeetStart with what they feel. Not what you think.
AmplifyMake it specific. A place, a number, something real.
TurnName the real cause. Not a scapegoat.
MoveShow them the law is on their side.
